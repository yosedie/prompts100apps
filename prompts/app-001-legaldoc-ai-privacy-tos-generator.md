## Application Name
LegalDoc AI: Privacy & ToS Generator

## Configuration & Attribution
*   **Target AI Model:** Gemini 2.5 Pro
*   **Creator:** yosedie

---

## Project Summary
Build a simple yet professional web application that functions as a basic legal document generator. Users enter the name and description of their service, and the app will generate a draft “Privacy Policy” and “Terms of Service.”

## User Interface Components (UI Components) (UI Components)

1.  **Header:** The large headline says "LegalDoc AI: Privacy Policy & T&C Generator".
2.  **User Input Form:**
    *   **Input Service Name:** A text input field labeled "Your Website/Application Name:".
    *   **Input Service Description:** A larger text area labeled "Describe Your Service:". Provide a placeholder such as "Example: A mobile application for sharing recipes, where users can register, upload photos, and leave comments."
3.  **Action Button:** A main button with the text "Create Document Now". While the process is running, this button should be disabled and display the status "Processing...".
4.  **Document Output Area:**
    *   **Important Disclaimer:** Directly above the results, display clear warning text: "**IMPORTANT:** This document was generated by AI and is a draft only. It is not legal advice. Always consult a legal professional for your specific needs."
    *   **Tab View:** Use the tabbed interface to separate the two documents.
        *   **Tab 1:** "Privacy Policy"
        *   **Tab 2:** "Terms & Conditions"
    *   **Document Content:** Each tab will contain a text area (read-only) displaying the resulting document. The text must be well formatted (paragraphs, headings, etc.).
    *   **Copy Button:** Each output area should have a "Copy Text" button in the upper right corner to make it easier for users to copy the entire document.
5.  **Footer:** A simple footer at the bottom of the page with the text "Created by yosedie".

## Workflow & Logic (Workflow & Logic)

1.  Users fill in the name and description of their service, then click the "Create Document Now" button.
2.  The application displays the loading status.
3.  In the background, the application creates a structured *prompt* to send to the AI ​​model. This prompt should follow the format:
    ```
    Anda adalah seorang asisten AI yang berspesialisasi dalam membuat draf dokumen legal dasar untuk layanan digital.

    Berdasarkan informasi berikut:
    - Nama Layanan: [Nama dari input pengguna]
    - Deskripsi Layanan: [Deskripsi dari input pengguna]

    Tugas Anda adalah menghasilkan DUA dokumen terpisah:
    1.  Draf Kebijakan Privasi (Privacy Policy).
    2.  Draf Syarat & Ketentuan (Terms of Service).

    Pastikan kedua dokumen tersebut komprehensif untuk layanan yang dijelaskan, mencakup poin-poin standar seperti pengumpulan data, penggunaan data, hak pengguna, batasan tanggung jawab, dll. Gunakan format yang jelas dengan judul dan sub-judul.

    Pisahkan kedua dokumen dengan jelas menggunakan penanda unik, misalnya: "---END PRIVACY POLICY---".
    ```
4.  The application sends this prompt to the **Gemini 2.5 Pro** model API.
5.  After receiving a response, the application parses the text, separating it into two documents based on unique markers.
6.  App displays Privacy Policy in Tab 1 and Terms & Conditions in Tab 2.
7.  The loading state is removed and the results are displayed.
